## Important Limitations

### 1. Backtesting ≠ Live Trading
- Historical performance ≠ guaranteed future performance
- Market conditions change
- Live execution may differ due to slippage, liquidity

### 2. Transaction Costs Not Included
- Broker commissions: ~0.1-0.5% per trade
- Bid-ask spreads: ~0.05-0.1% per trade
- **Net real-world return may be 0.15-0.65% lower per trade**

### 3. Data Quality Assumptions
- Assumes no missing data or errors
- Assumes accurate hourly OHLCV prices
- Real data may have gaps or errors

### 4. Model Drift
- Model trained on 3 months of historical data
- Market regimes change seasonally
- Need to retrain monthly/quarterly

### 5. Liquidity Constraints
- Analysis assumes can trade all 50 stocks easily
- Some stocks may have lower liquidity at certain times
- Large orders may move market prices
