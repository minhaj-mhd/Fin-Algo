# Live Implementation Mechanics: Veto Override Engine

## The Strategy
By enforcing a strict rule that the engine is only allowed to override vetoes using the highly profitable sub-clusters (>0.10% avg return), we capture a highly pure slice of trades.

* **Historical Trades Rescued:** 133 Trades out of 1,450
* **Frequency:** ~9.1% (Roughly 1 in every 10 vetoed signals is rescued)
* **Aggregate Win Rate:** 62.41%
* **Aggregate Average Net PnL (Unleveraged):** +0.184%
* **Aggregate Average Net PnL (5x Leverage & Slippage Adjusted):** +0.620% per trade (+82.45% Total Return)

## How the Live Engine Works
To make this work in a live production environment, we do not need the AI to run slow, heavy model predictions. We use pure, lightning-fast geometry. 

1. **The Feature Snapshot**: When a trade is generated by the engine, before it gets vetoed, the system grabs its 4 baseline features (`tech_score`, `nlp_sentiment`, `tv_sentiment`, `one_hour_prob`).
2. **The Scaling**: It passes those 4 numbers through the saved `scaler.pkl` file, translating the raw numbers into the exact same 4D mathematical space that the historical winners live in.
3. **The Distance Check**: The exact mathematical coordinates of the 5 "Sweet Spot" centroids are hard-coded directly into the Vanguard engine as arrays. The system calculates the pure Euclidean Distance from the incoming trade's scaled coordinates to those 5 sweet-spot coordinates.
4. **The Trigger**: If the distance to *any* of those 5 centroids is lower than its strict threshold (e.g., `< 0.50`), the engine immediately throws a flag: `VETO OVERRIDDEN: HIGH RETURN ARCHETYPE MATCH` and executes the trade.
