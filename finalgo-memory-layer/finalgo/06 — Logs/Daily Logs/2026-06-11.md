### 🔬 Dual-TF Entry/Exit Overlay Research (Conv-2026-06-11-DualTF-Entry-Exit-Research, Claude)
- **Status**: Concluded research arc (exploratory — no Gauntlet runs consumed; no verdict authority)
- **Question**: can the 15m conviction ranker dictate entry/exit/blocking for 1h top-3 signals?
- **Answer: NO — full-hold is optimal.** Five studies on a 6-fold purged WF panel (OOS 2024-09→2026-06, ~13k trades, 10 bps):
  1. **Entry rank-trajectory** (rising→take/falling→block): directionally right (+2.0/+2.4 bps) but ns (p≈0.22/0.28); orthogonal to price-momentum (corr≈0). **Later RETRACTED as a feature** — joint slope×level probe flipped the sign ordering (0/6 folds positive), so it's noise-level.
  2. **Conviction-decay early exit**: cuts losers −40→−23 bps but clips winners −24.6 (clip:save ≈ 1.4:1, anti-selective); net ≈ full-hold. Dumb price-stop is the *worst* rule (underwater trades recover).
  3. **Asymmetric "let winners run"** (exit only weak∧underwater): winner-clip collapses −24.6→−2.9 but loser-save collapses in lockstep → converges to full-hold. Full-hold = optimum of the entire {conviction, P&L} rule family.
  4. **Conviction-momentum × price-direction → remaining return** (user-designed): only significant result (long p=0.046) but **INVERTED** — "looks weak" (FAV−/CONV−) bounces +3.0, "looks strong" (FAV+/CONV+) exhausts −0.5; ~80% of the effect is the price mean-reversion axis.
  5. **Fresh probes**: depth-flat reversion (fixed ~2–3 bps refund regardless of dip size — "late dip entry" dead at −6.5/−8.2 net); ⭐ **EOD concentration finding** — hour-13 FAV− remaining return = **+6.7 bps long (p=0.0037) / +8.9 bps short (p=0.0066)** (14:45–15:15 window, coherent monotonic ramp both sides, matches known EOD/IBS book-squaring mechanism) — the closest any price/volume signal has come to the cost line, but still < 10 bps binding cost.
- **Synthesis**: residual price/volume edge = small, depth-insensitive, EOD-concentrated mean reversion; momentum/persistence framings inverted 4 independent ways (mechanistic: IBS/Buy_Pressure are mean-reversion detectors). Next edge requires new information (options OI/PCR/IV, order-flow) or lower friction. Two harness bugs caught & fixed mid-study (exit_rule_sweep `require_red`; exit_momentum_buckets short-side sgn).
- **Artifacts**: reusable panel `data/research/entry_exit/dualtf_trade_panel.csv` (13,020 trades × full 15m context; rules test in seconds), 6 new scripts in `scripts/analysis/`, 5 result logs + README with guardrails. Permanent note: [[04 — Research/Dual-TF Entry-Exit Overlay Research]].
